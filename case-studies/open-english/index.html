Summary: Open English is an online education academy for adults, children and businesses with a market leading SaaS e-learning platform and in LATAM providing live classes for english learning and on-demand content for students.

Traffic overview: 2.75M to 3.0M visits per month.

Year: 2017

Role: Product Manager

Project: Open English Redesign

The Pain Points:

  1. Site needed refresh and content updated to continue to attract users
  2. Lightweight design to improve download speeds, specially for mobile
  3. Find information easily about the Open English
  4. Show pricing information

Goals for project:

  • Pricing information (business decided, not to allow at the moment)
  • Develop pages for faster page download and accommodate LATAM mobile download speeds
  • Create content layout for easy desktop and mobile consumption
  • Have the ability to scale for new brands Junior and B2B

Final Preview: Live

Opportunities achieved:

  • Create a standard and scalable template for all Open English multi-language websites and new product offerings
  • Improved content consumption
  • Faster page speed for mobile traffic
  • Move easily throughout websites OE, Junior, B2B

Results:

Navigation was completely revamped and expanded for multi-site navigation. Lead form color scheme was updated to attract users. Increased lead conversion rate by 15% YOY. Page size decreased by 20% by compressing images and restructuring script files.
