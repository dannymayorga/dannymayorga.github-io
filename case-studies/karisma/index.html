Summary: Karisma Hotels & Resorts is a multi-branded premier hotel collection located in various countries such as Mexico, Jamaica, Dominican Republic, Colombia, Croatia and Serbia. It's been awarded several honors by Conde Nast Traveler’s, Forbes’ and Trip Advisor.

Traffic overview: 100k to 150k visits per month.

Year: 2014

Role: Web & UX Designer

The Pain Points:

  1. Navigation and display cannot scale for new hotel acquisitions.
  2. Homepage does not offer important information for users.
  3. Book Now button is not optimally placed for users to start process.

Goals for project:

  • Improve user experience and easily accessible information
  • Easy to navigate content
  • Drive guests to enter booking funnel
  • Accessible promotions and information on “Karisma Gourmet Inclusive” experience
  • Ability for the site / navigation to expand

Final Preview:

Opportunities achieved:

  • Navigation:
    • Easy accessible links for guests to check-in to hotel and for returning visitors
    • Organized navigation to display all available hotel destinations and offerings, social media links and language switcher
    • Positioned "Book Now" next to Specials link
  • “Karisma Gourmet Inclusive” / Booking Box:
    • Concise explanation about the Gourmet Inclusive concept
    • Booking Box expanded and up front for use showing specials or the ability to search vacations
    • Maintained booking box above the fold
  • Sales Module
    • Opportunity to see all promotions in one area
    • Provide toll-free number and consultant information
    • Newsletter sign-up banner
  • Footer
    • Expanded to show all brands and hotel collections

Results:

A great start to the redesign project which helped Karisma Hotels & Resorts obtain a site they may expand upon as they continue to grow through hotel acquisitions. They were thrilled to see the use of great photography and visual appeal. The inclusion of the "Gourmet Inclusive" concept information gives a welcomed overview for guests. Although, some features were not realized, content layout and overall design concept were achieved in the final design.
