Summary: Aeropost is an omni-channel e-commerce and logistics international company providing end-to-end, cross-border services and online marketplace for 35+ countries in Latin America. Idea generated to improve internal workflow for landing page creation. In addition, it provided the ability for individuals not familiar in designing or development to generate a landing page, quickly and easily.

Traffic overview: 1.25M visits per month.

Year: 2015

Role: Web & UX Designer

Project: Landing Page Digital App

The Pain Points:

  1. Workflow to create landing pages was not optimal for execution.
  2. Various campaign deadlines were passed due to workflow bottle necks.

Goals for project:

  • Build optimized workflow for internal users to generate landing pages
  • Provide options for different templates to choose from

App Preview URL: Demo

Note: This is a prototype, login button, navigation and pagination work.

Landing Page URL: Demo

Opportunities achieved:

  • Improved internal workflow, specifically for short deadline projects
  • Generator was responsive and could be used in desktop or mobile devices
  • Reduced deployment times for speedy campaign execution
  • As usage grew, more sections would be able to be automatized in future versions.

Results:

This project was conceptualized in about one week and prioritized by the technology team to implement. Using the most popular landing page design for lead conversion was essential to become customizable and generate rapidly. It was a great exercise to see the process of building applications and functionalities to improve production and for any type of user.
